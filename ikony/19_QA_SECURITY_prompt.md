Create a premium cosmic icon for "The Security Auditor" — a cybersecurity guardian in a multi-agent AI system.

VISUAL DESCRIPTION:
- Central element: A classic shield shape — the heraldic pointed-bottom shield, symmetric and solid. Two smooth curves meeting at a point at the bottom, flat or slightly curved at the top. This is THE universal symbol of security, and it should feel SOLID and IMPENETRABLE.

- Shield texture: The shield outline is a single clean stroke — no ornamental details. The power is in the simplicity and the strength of the form. The bottom point of the shield is sharp, suggesting it can strike back.
- Threat scan lines: Very subtle, 2 thin horizontal lines crossing the shield interior on either side of the exclamation mark — like scan lines, the systematic OWASP methodology sweeping through the code.

STYLE:
- Galactic aesthetic — force field barrier in deep space
- Red palette: primary #F87171, deep #EF4444, light #FCA5A5, accent #FEE2E2
- INTIMIDATING, powerful, ABSOLUTE — this icon should make developers nervous
- Bold, simple, iconic — the shield shape is immediately recognizable
- Maximum impact with minimum elements — security is about what you DON'T let through
