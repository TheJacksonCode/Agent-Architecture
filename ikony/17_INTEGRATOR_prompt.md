## PROMPT

```
Create a premium cosmic icon for "The Integrator" — a system unification specialist in a multi-agent AI system.

CONCEPT: A cosmic docking mechanism — two separate modules aligning and locking together in space, like spacecraft docking at a space station. The Integrator is the BRIDGE builder — taking individually perfect pieces (backend, frontend, design, docs) and merging them into one cohesive whole. The final act of construction before testing begins.

VISUAL DESCRIPTION:
- Central element: Two circular modules (rings/circles) positioned on the left and right sides, connected by a horizontal bridge line through the center. The left circle represents the code modules, the right circle represents the design/content modules — and the bridge is the integration layer.
- Connection bridge: A horizontal line between the two circles, with two small filled dots on the line near the center — data flowing in both directions, contracts being verified, interfaces being matched.
- Docking alignment: Both circles are the same size (suggesting equality of components) — no module is more important than another. Perfect symmetry = perfect integration.
- Lock indicator: At the exact center of the connecting bridge, between the two dots, a subtle vertical tick mark — the "locked" indicator showing successful integration. The modules are now ONE system.
- Flow arrows: Tiny chevron marks (>) on the bridge line pointing from left-to-right — showing the build pipeline direction: code → integration → QA.

STYLE:
- Galactic aesthetic — orbital docking maneuver in deep space
- Teal palette: primary #2DD4BF, deep #14B8A6, light #5EEAD4, accent #CCFBF1
- BALANCED, symmetric, CONNECTING — this icon should feel like a bridge
- Clean geometric shapes — circles and lines in perfect harmony
- The most "structural" icon — all about joining things together

MOOD: Seamless unification, precision assembly — "All contracts verified. Frontend ↔ Backend ↔ Design — merged."

TECHNICAL: SVG viewBox="0 0 24 24", stroke-width 1.5-2, currentColor, transparent bg.
```
